Workshop

We have a bike repair workshop every Wednesday starting at 18:00. If you want to come, check with us first, to be sure that someone will be here.

Brno bike (parts) shops

  • DIY bike shop in Brno run by Darek Galle - he has a lot of old frames and spare parts, ancient bikes too
  • Martian's favorite local bike mechanic: MIRASERVIS (Miloslav Raška), Hlinky 169/28, Brno, 543 210 700 open Mo-Fri 14:00 - 17:00
  • RK Cyklos, ~200m from base48, open Mo-Fri 09:00-12:00,13:00-18:00, Sat 09:00-12:00
