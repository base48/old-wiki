What

  • Reliable workhorse mounted in 21U rack

Heated chamber

  • http://reprap.org/wiki/Heated_Build_Chamber
  • heat source (need 40-80°C, typical operating temperature seems to be 50°C)
    • hair dryer
      • veverak's note: Personal experience is that hair dryer wasn't exactly happy that he had to run for a long time
    • hot air gun
    • light source
    • epic heatbed (e.g. 2x http://www.ebay.com/itm/40X40CM-1200W-220V-Huge-Mega-3D-Printer-Heatbed-Build-Plate-Silicone-Heater-Pad-/272059263635

)

    • combination of the above
  • SSR
  • insulation

Heatbed bed

  • custom size required
  • SSR

Frame

  • 2020 alu
  • V-wheels
  • Bolted to rack
  • Separate X/Y & Z stages
  • angle brackets
  • M6 hw

Motion

X/Y

Alternative I
  • Alumimaker 2 style
  • precision rods (M8)
  • precision bearings
  • shafts
  • gt2 belts
  • gt2 pulleys (20 teeth, 8mm bore)
  • 0.9° steppers
    • need to sustain heated bed temperature
Alternative II
  • supported rails
  • ballscrews

Z axis

  • 4x M12 rods
  • 4x LMK12LUU
  • 4x SHF12 12mm shaft support
  • ALU plate

Extruder

  • 2x bowden
    • drive wheel has to be located outside of the heated chamber
  • full metal
  • dual drive wheel

Hotend

  • dual or 2 separate X gantries
    • Cyclops (90GBP), needs water cooling mod
  • plastics
    • ABS
    • PLA
    • PVA support

Shoplift

Frame

  • (need) 3030 alu from http://ehlinik.cz

X/Y
