What

There's a mainboard in the Base that needs a replacement BIOS, let's try our soldering skills.. :) As a result, we could have a replacement for Evilbastard and a decent gaming/3D modeling computer too.

Please help supply the CPU (LGA775) to test it with.

Materials

I'm going to purchase this. --Martian742 18:00, 6 June 2012 (CEST)
