BaseQuad

Mapping quadcopter build.

Specs

  • Frame: HK X666
  • Motors: SunnySky X2212 980KV
  • ESC: Afro 20A Slim (FW BLHeli 14.1)
  • 10" Props
  • BAT: HK Nanotech 3S 6000mAh
  • FC: RTFHawk (PixHawk clone)
  • GPS: UBlox NEO 6M
  • RaspberryPi Zero + RPi Camera v1

Weights:

  • 1340g with 5000 zippy battery
  • 960g bare

Reconstruction

Done with OpenDroneMap.

  • https://github.com/OpenDroneMap/OpenDroneMap
  • https://github.com/mapillary/OpenSfM
  • https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Structure_from_motion

Results

Only one successful mapping flight performed so far with promising results.

Number of things will be improved in the future:

  • RPi camera calibration (OpenCV calibration)
  • Camera vibration isolation
  • GPS coordinates from flight controller fed into EXIF of pictures taken
