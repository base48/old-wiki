Label signs for our stuff:

given that I placed my labelmaker in base, there is now place to make labels for our stuff ... so, it makes sense to estabilish some code for this, I suggest we put logos here for specific things (like warning that something is fragile, something is flamable, that something shoudln't be used without briefing etc...) and also make A4 list with that logos and their description somewhere in base, or maybe on multiple places. Afaik this labels/logo are in some way allready existing? (I mean, we know that there are some logos for flamable, fragile and hazardous... also radioactive :) ) -> If somebody knows these things or knows where to find them I think that would be best approach for things that are allready designed. For other stuff I suppose we can create our own.

After we agree on something, I think we could print several of these logos in stock AND keep database of the logos somewhere (preferably on github) for reprinting
