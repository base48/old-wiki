During this weekend 8-9. 3. we are going to start assembling Shapeoko-2 and if time permits two 3D printers (kossel mini & prusa i3). Everyone is welcome to join us and help with the assembly or progress documentation (recording timelapses, taking picz).

  • http://docs.shapeoko.com/
  • http://reprap.org/wiki/Kossel
  • http://reprap.org/wiki/Prusa_i3

Wanted:

  • high-res cameras for timelapses
  • thin shrink tubes
  • soldering iron
  • beer (and/or other supplements)

Start times might differ, consult IRC.
