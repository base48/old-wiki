base48 TAZ

Donated by LulzBot as part of the 4th annual hackerspace giveaway [1]

  • TAZ 4 Manual (pdf

Parameters

  • Build envelope (fw limited) 298x275x250mm

Components

Bed

  • 24V 360W silicone heater from NPH
  • 3mm borosilicate
  • 100k Honeywell thermistor 135-104LAG-J01
  • 300x300mm

Hotend

  • E3D v6 Volcano, 0.8 mm hardened steel nozzle
  • 100K thermistor - ATC Semitec 104GT-2 (Used in ParCan & J-Head) (4.7k pullup)
  • 12V heater on 24V!

Firmware

  • https://github.com/hackerspace/newMarlin
    • base48_taz branch

Slic3r configs

  • https://github.com/hackerspace/slic3r

Usage

  • 250000 baudrate
  • changing Z offset:
    • M206 Z<new offset in mm>
  • changing E steps/mm
    • M92 E<new steps/mm>
  • save to EEPROM with M500
  • print current settings with M503
